Projects

Crunchy Choc Protein Bar: Food Safety Plan

Food Safety

Comprehensive food safety plan for a protein bar product

Project Overview

Developed a cereal-based protein bar with a balance of protein, fiber, and carbohydrates, designed to meet the nutritional needs of a broad consumer base. The product contains a mix of rice syrup, dark chocolate, milk proteins, wheat proteins, fibers, and pistachios.

Key Features

  • Low water activity (Aw 0.6-0.7) for shelf stability without artificial preservatives
  • pH level between 5.0-6.0 for product stability and pleasant taste
  • Individually wrapped in airtight, moisture-resistant packaging
  • Shelf-life of 12-18 months under proper storage conditions
  • Allergen control system for milk, soy, nuts, and wheat

Skills Demonstrated

  • Comprehensive HACCP plan development
  • Hazard analysis for biological, physical, and allergen risks
  • Balancing nutritional value, taste, and texture with food safety requirements
  • Application of Australian food safety regulations
